What you should know about Boiler Upgrades

With Boilers it's kind of like finding your wand before wizarding school; the Boiler chooses you, or at least your home. You may not realize this but choosing a new Boiler has a lot to do with the old system that we're replacing. Some older Boiler Systems have old cast iron radiators that aren't compatible with some of the newer systems. This means either choosing a more specific Boiler for your home or upgrading your radiator equipment to meet today's Energy Efficient standards. Part of the installation process is examining your current system's faults and how we can improve on them like eliminating cold spots.

The Boiler Kings are the leading Ottawa Valley experts in Boiler Installation, Maintenance, Service and Repairs. Our unparralled service and 100% customer satisfaction guarantee will have you sleeping comfortably knowing your indoor climate is totally under your control! We install a wide range of residential Boiler Systems using different energy sources like Gas, Oil and Propane and can even customize your climate control for multiple zones. 

Factors to Consider When Upgrading your Boiler System:

1) How the new Boiler will improve your climate control
2) The Canadian Safety Standards for installing Boilers in Ottawa homes
3) The Boiler's dependability and accessability to replacement parts
4) If the new Boiler will work with old cast iron rads or if newer equipment needs to be installed
5) The Boiler's Energy Efficiency & how much the new Heating System will save you on your next energy bill
6) Affordability and Financing or Rental options
